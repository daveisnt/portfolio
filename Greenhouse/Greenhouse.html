My Greenhouse Project

What a topsy-turvy world we found ourselves in, as the COVID pandemic picked up steam and the summer of 2020 was fast approaching. And what better time, I thought, to build a greenhouse! Jess had always wanted a greenhouse and with my new job in agriculture and new found time due to pandemic lockdowns, there couldn't have been a better time. The basic idea was this:

  • Build a greenhouse to extend our growing season
  • Incorporate a system that measures temperatures at different locations over time
  • Design a temperature regulation system that opens and closes hatches according to current temperature
  • Make it accessible via a simple webapp

We started in the early spring with some measurements of the house and yard. From there, I outsourced the structural design to my dad who produced a fine set of blueprints plus a materials list. We were off to a great start!
